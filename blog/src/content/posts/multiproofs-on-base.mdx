In particular, every block in a proposal is proved to be correct.</p><p>These systems solve some of the issues optimistic systems face, such as:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Non-Interactive Process</strong>: Proofs are generated offchain and verified onchain in a single transaction, no longer needing to provide time for participants to perform several steps.
